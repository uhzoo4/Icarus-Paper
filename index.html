If you are the sun —

then I must be Icarus.

For I know I’m bound to fall.

And I may even drown.

But despite it all —

I’ll smile the whole way down.

It’s worth it to burn

when it’s coming from you.

If it isn’t my turn —

I’m proud that I flew.
